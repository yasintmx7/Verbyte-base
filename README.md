**Verbyte** simulates a high-stakes terminal intrusion where players must decipher a hidden password (word) before their system integrity reaches zero.

Unlike traditional blockchain games that require a transaction for every move, Verbyte uses a **"Play Local, Verify Global"** architecture:

1.  **Session Mode**: Gameplay happens instantly in your browser (Off-Chain). No lag, no gas fees per guess.
2.  **Proof of Victory**: Only when you succeed do you sign a transaction to commit your win to the Base blockchain.
3.  **Multiplayer Sync**: Share a Room Seed with a friend to play the same puzzle, then sync your results on-chain to see who did better.
